My Journey

Future Aspirations

I envision a future where technology becomes a bridge that solves real problems and empowers everyday people. Currently, I am gaining hands-on experience during my internship at the Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA), where I am learning how IT systems support large-scale operations, service delivery, and data management. This experience has strengthened my passion for building reliable, user-centered digital solutions. One of my biggest aspirations is to develop an all-in-one business software — a platform that will streamline daily operations, from sales and inventory to customer management and analytics. I want to create a system that is affordable, simple to use, and truly transformative for small and medium-sized businesses across Kenya and beyond. Academically, I dream of completing my degree with a Second Class Upper Honours or higher. Achieving this distinction is part of my personal commitment to discipline, growth, and excellence. In the long run, I hope to establish my own tech firm, a space dedicated to innovation, mentorship, and solving African challenges through modern technology. I want to contribute meaningful solutions while creating opportunities for young developers who share the same vision.

Roadmap

  1. Complete my degree with a strong GPA (Second Class Upper or better). Strengthen my knowledge through hands-on projects and research.

  2. Gain real industry experience during my internship at KRA. Secure additional placements or junior roles in IT/software development. Build a solid professional network in Kenya’s tech ecosystem.

  3. Volunteer in tech communities and mentor younger students who are starting out. Improve communication, teamwork, and leadership skills. Stay updated on industry trends and continuously learn.
